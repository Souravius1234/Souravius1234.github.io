About Me

I am an Aerospace Engineer from India, currently a Graduate Student at the Intelligent Space Systems Laboratory, Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics at the University of Tokyo. I designed this webpage taking inspiration from Interstellar, a movie which motivated me to this path towards exploring the cosmos. I study and do research in the areas of Space Debris Tracking, Propagation, and Risk Assessment in the Cislunar Region, Trajectory Design and Optimization, Mission Design and Operations, Embedded Systems, and Robotics.

I am not a web-designer, and have tried my best to improve this webpage. In fact, I made this website to kill some boredom, and also because I felt that my previous webpage wasn't looking that great. I am open to suggestions, so please don't hesitate to reach out to me via the contact form or via my socials!
The code to this webpage is available on my Github. Please do drop by and take a look!
To know more about me, check out my About Me page!
